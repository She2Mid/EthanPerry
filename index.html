Hobbies

I am a gamer and a member of my school's JROTC program. On the surface, these two passions may seem vastly different, but they both bring me a sense of purpose and community. Through gaming, I have found a network of like-minded individuals who share my love for competition and virtual adventures. In JROTC, I have discovered a sense of discipline and camaraderie that comes with being part of a team. While my time spent gaming may seem frivolous to some, I see it as a way to unwind and recharge after a long day of school and training. At the end of the day, whether I'm leading a virtual raid or marching in formation with my fellow cadets, I feel a sense of fulfillment and pride in the communities I am a part of.
